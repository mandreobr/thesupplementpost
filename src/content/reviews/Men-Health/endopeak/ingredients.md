<h3>Key Active Ingredients</h3>

<div class="ingredient-card-list">
  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>1. Tribulus Terrestris</h3>
    <p>
      Traditionally associated with libido, sexual motivation, and vitality. It is commonly used to support arousal readiness and male confidence, particularly when low desire is connected to stress, fatigue, or age-related hormonal shifts.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>2. Chrysin (Oroxylum indicum)</h3>
    <p>
      A flavonoid included for oxidative balance and vascular responsiveness. Antioxidant support matters because oxidative strain can reduce endothelial function — a key factor behind healthy blood flow and erectile performance.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>3. Epimedium (Horny Goat Weed)</h3>
    <p>
      Known for icariin-like compounds that support nitric oxide signaling and circulation. This is directly relevant to erection firmness and endurance, because stronger blood flow is one of the most consistent predictors of erection quality.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>4. Tongkat Ali (Eurycoma longifolia)</h3>
    <p>
      Often used to support libido, energy, and stress resilience. In male performance formulas, tongkat ali is typically included to help maintain a healthier hormonal rhythm and improve vitality over time rather than delivering short-term stimulation.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>5. Saw Palmetto</h3>
    <p>
      Included to support male hormonal balance and overall reproductive health. Many men-focused formulas use saw palmetto as part of a broader strategy aimed at maintaining steadier male wellness as age-related changes accumulate.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>6. Hawthorn Berry</h3>
    <p>
      Traditionally used for cardiovascular support, with relevance to circulation and vascular tone. Because erections are fundamentally a blood-flow event, hawthorn is positioned as a supportive ingredient for men who want performance support anchored in healthier circulation.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>7. Cissus Quadrangularis</h3>
    <p>
      Used in wellness contexts for physical resilience and recovery. In EndoPeak’s formula, cissus is positioned to complement stamina and endurance goals, especially for men who feel performance is affected by low energy or slower recovery.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>8. Magnesium (as Magnesium Oxide)</h3>
    <p>
      Essential for muscle and nerve function, sleep quality, and energy metabolism — all relevant to sexual performance. When sleep and recovery suffer, libido and erection quality often follow, which is why magnesium is frequently included in male vitality formulas.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>9. Zinc (as Zinc Oxide)</h3>
    <p>
      A foundational mineral for male hormonal health and reproductive function. Zinc is closely tied to libido and testosterone-related processes, making it a practical inclusion for men seeking steady, long-term sexual vitality support.
