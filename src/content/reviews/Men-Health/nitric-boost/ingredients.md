<p>
  These amino acids are widely researched for their roles in nitric oxide synthesis and endothelial function. Supporting nitric oxide production is directly relevant to erectile physiology because nitric oxide signaling helps relax blood vessels and promote sufficient blood flow for firmer erections.

This aligns with Nitric Boost’s “multi-pathway” approach rather than relying on a single nitric oxide ingredient alone.
