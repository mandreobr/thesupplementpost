<h2 id="key-ingredients">Key TC24 Ingredients Explained</h2>

<p>
  Below is a practical overview of TC24’s core ingredients and why they were selected for a urinary and prostate-support formula.
</p>

<div class="ingredient-card-list">
  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>1. Pine Pollen Extract (500 mg)</h3>
    <p>
      Pine pollen is rich in phyto-compounds and antioxidant elements that help reinforce cellular defense and inflammation support. In urinary formulas, these effects are relevant because oxidative pressure and tissue irritation can influence long-term comfort.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>2. Saw Palmetto (300 mg)</h3>
    <p>
      Saw palmetto is one of the most widely recognized prostate botanicals. It is commonly discussed for supporting male hormone metabolism (including DHT-related pathways) and urinary comfort — especially for men experiencing gradual changes in flow and nighttime patterns.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>3. Pygeum Africanum (200 mg)</h3>
    <p>
      Pygeum contains phytosterols and triterpenes associated with prostate tissue comfort. It is frequently referenced in discussions around urinary flow support, bladder emptying comfort, and nighttime urination patterns.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>4. Jaboticaba Skin Extract (100 mg)</h3>
    <p>
      Jaboticaba is a Brazilian fruit rich in polyphenols. In the context of urinary and prostate health, polyphenols are valued for antioxidant support that helps protect tissues from oxidative stress, supporting resilience and long-term function.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="ingredient-card">
    <h3>5. Lipophytol®</h3>
    <p>
      Lipophytol® is positioned as a circulation-support compound. Because pelvic microcirculation influences nutrient delivery and tissue responsiveness, circulation support can be relevant for long-term urinary comfort and vitality.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

<p>
  <strong>Additional Ingredients:</strong> Tocopheryl nicotinate (vitamin E derivative), neem leaf powder, and boron — included to support circulation efficiency, antioxidant protection, microbial balance support, and male hormone metabolism.
</p>

<h2 id="science">Confirming What Is Scientifically Known About the Ingredients</h2>

<p>
While Total Control 24 itself has not been evaluated in large-scale clinical trials, its formulation is built around ingredients that have been individually studied for their roles in prostate health, urinary function, circulation, inflammation balance, and hormonal regulation.
</p>

<p>
Pygeum Africanum has been evaluated in multiple human studies for lower urinary tract symptoms associated with prostate enlargement, with research indicating improvements in urine flow rate, reduced nighttime urination, and better bladder emptying through inflammation balance within prostate tissues.
</p>

<p>
Saw Palmetto is one of the most extensively researched botanical ingredients for prostate support, with scientific literature linking it to healthy DHT metabolism, reduced prostate tissue sensitivity, and improvements in urinary comfort, flow consistency, and urgency.
</p>

<p>
Jaboticaba Polyphenols are rich in anthocyanins and antioxidant compounds shown to defend prostate cells against oxidative stress, a key factor in age-related tissue decline and inflammation.
</p>

<p>
Lipophytol® is associated with enhanced microcirculation and improved delivery of oxygen and nutrients into prostate tissues, supporting metabolic efficiency and urinary function as circulation naturally declines with age.
</p>

<p>
Boron plays a documented role in hormonal regulation by supporting testosterone availability and metabolic balance, which are important for maintaining prostate tissue stability and reducing sensitivity to inflammatory stimuli.
</p>

<p>
Neem and vitamin E derivatives, including tocopheryl nicotinate, contribute antimicrobial and antioxidant activity that supports cellular protection, tissue resilience, and a balanced urinary environment.
</p>

<p>
Together, these mechanisms address inflammation, oxidative stress, circulation efficiency, and hormonal balance, forming a coherent, evidence-informed rationale for Total Control 24’s role as a long-term prostate support formula.
</p>

<h2 id="symptoms">Prostate &amp; Urinary Health — Symptoms That Indicate It’s Time to Pay Attention</h2>

<p>
  Urinary changes can present gradually — and ignoring them often leads to worsening sleep disruption and daily discomfort. Many men first notice weak stream strength, increased frequency, urgency, or nighttime interruptions.
</p>

<p><strong>Common signs men experience include:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Weaker or slower urine flow</li>
  <li>Difficulty initiating urination</li>
  <li>Repeated nighttime trips to the bathroom</li>
  <li>A feeling of incomplete emptying</li>
  <li>Pelvic or lower-abdominal tension</li>
  <li>Sudden urgency from small fluid intake</li>
  <li>Changes in energy, stamina, or motivation</li>
</ul>

<p>
  TC24 is designed to support underlying pathways associated with these symptoms, particularly inflammation balance, antioxidant protection, circulation support, and hormone metabolism.
